# Carousel Creation Guide — 2026 Best Practices (Research-Backed)

## Why Carousels Win in 2026

Data from Socialinsider 2025 + 2026 algorithm research:

| Format | Engagement vs Single Image |
|--------|---------------------------|
| 8-10 slides | **3.15x higher** |
| 4-7 slides | 1.8x higher |
| 2-3 slides | Barely better |
| <4 slides | No significant boost |

- **Reach:** 1.4x more than single image (algorithm resurfaces slides to non-engagers)
- **Saves:** Highest save rate of any format (signals lasting value)
- **Dwell time:** Algorithm measures time spent — carousels win here
- **DM Shares:** Carousels with reference value get shared most (strongest 2026 signal)

## Optimal Slide Count

| Goal | Slides | Notes |
|------|--------|-------|
| Simple comparison/before-after | 2-3 | Minimum viable |
| Quick tips list | 4-5 | For simple advice |
| Tutorial / How-to | 7-10 | **Sweet spot** |
| Deep dive, comprehensive | 11-15 | Use sparingly (drop-off risk) |
| **Best overall** | **8-10** | Highest engagement per post |

Instagram allows 20 slides now (2024 update) — but sweet spot stays 8-10.

## Slide-by-Slide Structure (10 Slides)

### Slide 1 — Hook (CRITICAL)
**Goal:** Stop scroll in 2 seconds

✓ Bold promise or stat
✓ Surprising claim
✓ Relatable pain point written as a question
✓ Specific numbers beat vague ("14 hours" not "hours")

✗ NO logo or brand name on this slide
✗ NO vague titles ("My journey" — what journey?)
✗ NO context buried in small text

**Examples:**
- "3 mistakes you're making with AI automation..."
- "Stop doing [X]. Here's what to do instead."
- "14 hours/week. That's what I saved. Here's how."
- "You won't believe what happened when..."

### Slide 2 — Problem Agitation
**Goal:** Make them feel understood

- "You know that feeling when..."
- State the problem they experience
- Validate their frustration
- NO solution yet

**Keep it to:** 1 headline + 2-3 lines

### Slide 3 — The Turn (Transition)
**Goal:** Bridge from problem to solution

- "Here's what I used to do..." → "Here's what changed..."
- "That was until I discovered..."
- "Most people think... But actually..."

### Slides 4-7 — Value Delivery (pick 4)
**Goal:** Teach the thing, one idea per slide

- 1 headline per slide (max 5-7 words)
- 2-3 supporting sentences
- Icon or simple visual per slide
- White space = readability
- Examples with specific numbers

**Structure options for these slides:**
- Step-by-step breakdown
- Before/after comparison
- List format (3-5 points)
- Quote + explanation
- Tool/method explanation

### Slide 8 — Social Proof
**Goal:** Validate the promise you made on slide 1

- Client quote (real or archetypal)
- Specific result with numbers
- Before/after stat

### Slide 9 — Key Takeaway
**Goal:** Give them something to screenshot/save

- Recap of the main lesson
- Single powerful line
- Checklist or summary format

### Slide 10 — CTA (CRITICAL)
**Goal:** Trigger action — double CTA

**Double CTA formula:**
- "Save this for later" (saves)
- "Comment [word] and I'll send you [X]" (comments)
- "Tag someone who needs this" (shares/DM)
- "Follow for more [topic]" (followers)

**Strong close:**
- "This took me 6 months to learn. You just got it free."
- "Your turn. Link in bio."
- "Which step are you trying first? Drop it below."

## Design Rules

### Aspect Ratio
- **1080x1350 (4:5)** — most common, highest engagement
- **1080x1080 (1:1)** — also good, grid-friendly

### Typography
- 1 font, 2 weights (bold headline + regular body)
- Headlines: bold, 40-60pt minimum
- Body: regular, 24-36pt minimum
- Readable at thumbnail size (test it)

### Color & Visual
- Same background per carousel (brand consistency)
- Appie as visual hero where possible
- High contrast for accessibility
- Simple icons, consistent style

### White Space
- Don't fill every pixel
- Ample white space = scanable content
- "Less is more" for each slide

## 2026 Algorithm Optimization

### Save Triggers (Slide 9-10)
- "Save this for [specific situation]"
- Checklists and templates
- "Screenshots this" worth content
- Step-by-step guides

### Share Triggers (Slide 1)
- "Tag someone who..."
- "Send this to your [friend/colleague] who..."
- Universal problem (anyone with this problem shares)

### Comment Triggers (Throughout)
- Questions embedded in slides
- "Which would you choose? A or B?"
- "Comment your answer below"
- "What's your biggest struggle with [topic]?"

## NL + EN Carousel Strategy

### Process
1. Create carousel in EN first
2. Adapt hook + body for NL
3. Same visual (same file)
4. Separate posts, same day
5. NL morning, EN afternoon

### Caption Differences
- NL: More direct, punchy
- EN: More expansive, "the", "your"
- Same hashtags NOT mixed per language
- Same slide structure for both

### Scheduling
- **Carousel format** best for midweek (Wed-Thu peak engagement)
- Morning slots: 11:00-12:00 CET (lunch browsing)
- Evening slots: 18:00-19:00 CET
