# Caption Framework — 2026 Instagram Formulas

## 2026 Algorithm Priorities (Update Your Thinking)

1. **Sends/DM Shares** — #1 signal in 2026 (3-5x more weight than likes)
2. **Saves** — #2 signal (educational, reference content gets saved most)
3. **Comments** — #3 signal (drive with questions, opinions, CTAs)
4. **Likes** — still matter but distant 4th
5. **Watch time** — top for Reels, not Feed

**Capiton strategy implications:**
- Write captions that people want to DM to a friend
- Include explicit "send to someone who..." prompts
- Design content that has lasting reference value (gets saved)
- Ask questions that drive comments

## Carousel Slide Formulas

### Slide 1 — Hook (CRITICAL)
**Purpose:** Stop scroll in 2 seconds
- Bold promise, surprising stat, or specific pain point
- NO logo, NO brand name on hook slide
- Text: max 5-7 words large, readable at thumbnail
- Visual: high contrast, Appie as hero

### Slides 2-3 — Problem Agitation
**Purpose:** Make them feel understood
- "You know that feeling when..."
- State the problem they recognize
- NO solution yet

### Slides 4-7 — The Shift (Solution)
**Purpose:** Show the path
- What most people do wrong
- What actually works
- Transition from problem to solution

### Slides 8-9 — Value Delivery
**Purpose:** Teach the thing
- 1 idea per slide
- Step-by-step breakdown
- Examples or proof

### Slide 10 — Social Proof + CTA
**Purpose:** Validate + trigger action
- Quote or specific result
- Double CTA: "Save this" + "Comment [word]"
- Strong closing line

## Caption Length Guidelines (2026)
- **150-300 words** for carousels (enough for algorithm keywords, story)
- **50-125 words** for Reels (watch time is the metric)
- **Short punchy** for single image (scroll stopper needs to be fast)

## Caption SEO (Keywords)
Instagram uses multimodal AI — captions with keyword-rich language help categorization ~30% more than hashtags alone.
- Include natural language for your topic
- Write like you speak
- Keywords in first 125 characters matter most

## Engagement Velocity Rules
- Reply to comments within first hour (strongest signal)
- Engage with your own comments before responding to others
- Edit caption within first 30 minutes if needed
- First comment with hashtags = clean caption (but same engagement value)

## NL + EN Bilingual Rules
- Same visual, different caption per language
- Separate posts, same day
- NL: morning (09:00-11:00 CET)
- EN: afternoon/evening (18:00-20:00 CET)
- Never mix hashtags per language
