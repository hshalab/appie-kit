## Content Format Strategy

### Carousel Strategy (Primary Format)
- **8-10 slides** = highest engagement
- Hook in slide 1 (stop scroll in 2 sec)
- Double CTA in slide 10 ("Save" + "Comment")
- NL+EN same day, separate posts
- Reference: `references/carousel-guide.md`

### Reels Strategy
- **Structure:** Hook (0-3s) → Tension (3-10s) → Payload (10-25s) → CTA (25-30s)
- Hook in first frame with text overlay
- Aim for 70%+ completion rate
- Hook-first scripting (71% of viewers decide in 3 sec)
- Reference: `references/reels-script.md`

### Single Image Strategy
- For quotes, stats, quick authority posts
- High-contrast visual with Appie
- Punchy caption (50-125 words)
