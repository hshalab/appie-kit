# Atlas Growth Desk session notes

Origin: user asked to build a side hustle with Spark Atlas as operator/driver, target €30k-€50k/month, preferably inside existing sectors and using Eva/Appie-kit/knowledge base where available.

## Strategic conclusion

Avoid random side hustles. Start with a high-ticket B2B productized service in existing advantage zones:

- Dubai/international real estate
- Dutch Realtor investor guidance
- recruitment/staffing
- sales coaching/enablement
- AI automation/content operations

## First business model

`Atlas Growth Desk`: AI-operated growth ops for real estate, recruitment, and sales teams.

Core deliverable: done-for-you AI lead generation + content + outreach + follow-up + CRM + reporting.

## Pilot offer

Name: `30 Dagen Lead Engine Sprint`

Price:

- €1,500-€3,000 setup/pilot
- then €2,500-€5,000/month retainer
- optional performance bonus for qualified meetings/deals

Deliverables:

- positioning audit
- ICP and prospect list
- 20 outreach messages
- 30-day content calendar
- 12 posts minimum
- lead magnet hooks
- follow-up scripts
- CRM pipeline
- weekly report
- sales-call prep

## Revenue target math

- 10 clients × €3k/month = €30k MRR
- 15 clients × €3k/month = €45k MRR
- 5 clients × €5k/month + 10 smaller clients = €40k-€50k/month

## Operational boundaries

Spark can research, draft, build lists, create assets, automate CRM/reporting, and prep sales. External outreach, public posting, ad spend, client commitments, contracts, and use of private data require explicit approval unless later delegated.
