# Weblyfe editorial voice and cadence

Captured from the May 24, 2026 session.

## Non-negotiables
- Publish 2 blogs per week.
- Build posts from market research plus internal learnings from Weblyfe work.
- Write in Seyed's voice: direct, authentic, no corporate fluff.
- Do not use em dashes.
- Use good sources and backlinks.
- Prefer decision-stage and commercial-intent topics when they fit the cluster.

## LinkedIn adaptation
- LinkedIn posts can be written in Dutch when requested.
- Keep the voice natural and concise, not corporate.

## Practical framing
- Start with the best learnings from the archive and current market signals.
- Make each post useful on its own, but also part of a cluster.
- Pair every blog with a clear internal-link path back to Weblyfe pages.
- Treat the Notion blog database `Blog Articles Weblyfe` as the CMS source of truth for blog drafts and completed articles.
- When the same idea is repurposed into LinkedIn copy, Dutch is acceptable if requested.
