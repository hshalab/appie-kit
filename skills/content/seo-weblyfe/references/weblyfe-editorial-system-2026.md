# Weblyfe editorial system, May 2026

Session-derived notes for the Weblyfe blog program.

## Cadence
- Publish 2 posts per week.
- Plan from keyword/topic clusters, not isolated ideas.
- Use research first, then briefs, then drafting.

## Current cluster directions
- Story-driven web design and conversion
- Webflow vs WordPress and platform choice
- AI workflows, agents, and agency automation

## Practical rules
- Give each post one primary keyword and one cluster role.
- Prefer commercial-intent topics that support service pages.
- Interlink new posts to the strongest existing page in the cluster.
- Refresh older posts when they can be strengthened into cluster pillars or support pieces.

## Observed archive tendencies
- Existing archive leans toward AI/automation, Webflow comparison content, story-driven design, founder authority, and some local SEO.
- The strongest posts are decision-stage and commercial investigation pieces.
