## The Hormozi rule: one keyword per phrase

Direct quote from Ascynd analysis:
**"For each phrase, identify the single most meaningful word and change its fill from white to yellow. This is the highest-leverage manual step."**

The error beginners make: highlighting every other word. This defeats the purpose. The highlighted word is an anchor for the viewer's eye — if everything is anchored, nothing is.

**Selection rule:** Pick the single noun or verb that carries the semantic weight. Not the adjective, not the article, not the connector.

Examples:
- "I built a full WhatsApp system" → highlight "WhatsApp" (specific/memorable) or "built" (action verb)
- "in just a few hours" → highlight "hours" (specificity)
- "it's currently happening" → highlight "happening" (stakes)
- "10X your productivity" → highlight "10X" (number/promise)

## What categories of words deserve highlight

From reelwords.ai — words that do one of these jobs:

**KEEP (highlight these):**
- Promise: "free", "fast", "in minutes", "instantly", "10X"
- Contrast: "but", "instead", "here's the trick", "never", "unlike"
- Specificity: numbers, timeframes, outcomes, proper nouns (product names, platform names)
- Stakes: "wasting", "losing", "missing", "costing"

**SKIP (never highlight these):**
- Function words: "the", "and", "a", "of", "in", "to"
- Hedges: "like", "just", "really", "basically", "kind of"
- Repeated emphasis on every line — "If everything is emphasized, nothing is emphasized"
- Emphasis that does not match the spoken energy

## Color specifications

Standard (business/education/creator content):
- Primary: Yellow — `#FFD93D` or `#FFEE33`
- Alternative green: `#39FF14` or `#A6FF00`

Platform-specific alternatives (from canvasub.com):
- Tech content: Cyan `#00FFFF`
- Finance: Green `#00FF88`
- Lifestyle: Pink `#FF69B4`

**Rule:** Any muted or pastel color breaks the effect. High saturation and high contrast against white text is required.

---

## Timing requirements

- Word-level timestamp accuracy: **within 100ms** (canvasub.com: "This is what separates professional karaoke captions from amateur ones")
- Timing per word: 200–500ms (varies with speech speed)
- Words must NOT appear before they are spoken — this is the most common amateur error

How to verify: watch the first 5 seconds at 0.5x speed. Every word should appear exactly on the syllable, not 1–2 frames early.

---

## Layout specifications (for 9:16 vertical video)

- Font: Heavy condensed sans-serif (Montserrat Black, Anton, Bebas Neue)
- Case: ALL-CAPS
- Stroke: thick black outline on white letters
- Size: 42–56px at 1080x1920 resolution
- Lines per display: 3–5 words maximum per line, 2 lines max at once
- Position: lower-middle of screen (~60–70% down from top)
- Coverage: 10–15% of screen height per caption line

## Caption style by content type (captions.ai)

| Content type | Style | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Education/business | Hormozi Bold (word-by-word, yellow highlight) | Emphasizes key concepts for retention |
| Lifestyle/aesthetic | Minimal white | Keep visual focus on footage |
| Entertainment/fast-paced | Pop-up animated | Adds energy to cuts |
| Podcast clips | Single word or short phrase, clean font | Readability over flair |
| LinkedIn/professional | Clean, minimal or no animation | Subtlety signals credibility |

## The "active word highlight" principle

The currently spoken word should be emphasized (different color, size, or background) while inactive words remain neutral. This creates visual motion that holds attention on sound-off viewing and helps viewers follow along.

This is one of the highest-impact caption features. The motion itself is an attention hook, independent of the content.
