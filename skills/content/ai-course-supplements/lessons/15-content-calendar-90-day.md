# 90-day launch calendar — fresh aged IG to first $1k month

## Why this lesson exists

Herman teaches each step in isolation. He says "warm up your IG" (Phase 3) but the V2 video opens with "don't fret too much about little details." He says "post one reel per day" (Reels Masterclass) then later updates to "you have two options" (Phase 4) without committing to dates. The Fanvue module is its own island.

A new operator gets 13 hours of video and zero idea of what to physically do on Day 23. This lesson is that calendar — week by week, day-level checklist. Calibrated against the 14-day Warm Start research, ShadowPhone's 2026 Instagram warmup limits, AI Influencer Posting Frequency 2026, and the 2026 Instagram algorithm updates.

## What's the 2026 state of the art

Twelve weeks divided into four phases. The hard rules:

- **Phase 1 (Week 1-2): Warmup.** No link-in-bio, no Reels yet, no automation. Failing this is the #1 reason new creators get permanent shadowbans. The 14-day Warm Start data: rushing to under 5 days = ~73% higher shadowban risk; proper warmup = 3.4x better Explore placement at 90 days, 5x faster year-1 growth. https://campgroundsocial.com/blog/warm-start-sequence
- **Phase 2 (Week 3-4): Reels ramp.** Introduce Reels at 1/day, scale to 3/day by end of Week 4. AI Influencer Posting Frequency Guide 2026 puts the sweet spot at 1-3/day for AI creators; PostEverywhere's algo data caps growth-phase ROI at ~3 reels/day. https://aicreatorworld.net/ai-influencer-posting-frequency-guide/ · https://www.posteverywhere.ai/blog/how-often-to-post-on-social-media
- **Phase 3 (Week 5-6): Funnel goes live.** Link-in-bio, Bouncy AI, ManyChat comment-to-DM trigger.
- **Phase 4 (Week 7-12): Scale.** Paid Meta ads, Tinder Passport seeding, customs, ambassador application.

## How to set it up on Spark (calendar)

### Week 1 — Cold start (aged IG, fresh persona)

Daily limits per ShadowPhone 2026 schedule (extreme caution first 72 hours):

- **Day 1**: Complete profile (bio, 3 highlights, 9 grid posts pre-staged). Browse only. Like 10-15 niche posts. Follow 5 accounts. No DMs.
- **Day 2**: 1 grid post (lifestyle, SFW, no Fanvue mention). Like 15-20. Follow 5 more. Watch 5 Reels to completion.
- **Day 3**: 1 Story (poll or question, low-effort). Like 20. Comment 2-3 thoughtful replies on niche posts.
- **Day 4-5**: 1 grid post each, 1 Story, like 30, comment 5, follow 8-10. Reply to any DMs received.
- **Day 6-7**: 2 grid posts total over weekend, 2 Stories/day, like 40-50, comment 8, follow 10-12.

End of Week 1: ~7 grid posts, ~5 Stories, account has "warm" engagement signals. Trust score (per ShadowPhone) climbs out of cold-start.

### Week 2 — Building rhythm

- **Day 8-10**: Add 1 carousel/day. Stories 3x/day. Likes 50-70/day. Comments 10/day. Follow 12-15/day. First DM conversations (reply, not initiate).
- **Day 11-12**: Test 1 Reel (face-only, SFW, original audio). Watch performance for 48 hours. Engage every comment within 1 hour. If reach > follower count, the algorithm is recommending you — proceed.
- **Day 13-14**: 2 more Reels (different formats). Daily Story rhythm. End Week 2 with: 14+ grid pieces, 20+ Stories, 3 Reels, ~150-250 followers gained, account passes ShadowPhone "trust threshold."

### Week 3-4 — Reels ramp + format library

The Reels Masterclass formats Herman teaches are 6 base formats. In two weeks you build 18 reels (6 formats x 3 each) and learn which one your niche actually engages with.

- **Day 15-21**: 1 Reel/day. Grid: 3 posts/week. Stories: 3-5/day, mix of polls / questions / behind-scenes / GRWM-style. Likes 80-100/day. Follows 15-20/day.
- **Day 22-28**: 2 Reels/day Mon-Fri, 1/day weekends (10 reels in week). Format mix: 2x talking-head, 2x lifestyle B-roll, 2x trending-audio dance, 1x behind-the-scenes, 1x reaction, 1x narrative micro-story, 1x bonus.
- End of Week 4: 18 reels live, top 3 reels identified by view-through-rate and saves-to-likes ratio (target ≥1:4 per Warm Start data). Lock those formats as your core.

### Week 5-6 — Funnel switches on

- **Day 29**: Add link-in-bio. Use Bouncy.ai (single-link rotator with multi-click anti-fingerprint behavior). Configure: link 1 = Fanvue free preview, link 2 = ManyChat opt-in. https://seemyprofiles.com/
- **Day 30-31**: Configure ManyChat Quick Automation "Auto-DM links from comments" on top 3 Reels. Trigger keyword: niche-specific (e.g. "menu", "drop", "link"). https://help.manychat.com/hc/en-us/articles/16654065283100-Quick-Automation-Auto-DM-links-from-comments
- **Day 32-35**: Push 3 Reels/day with comment-bait CTA ("comment 'menu' for the link"). Watch DM conversion. Target 8-15% comment-to-DM, 30-50% DM-to-Fanvue-tap, 1.5-4% link-to-paid-sub (per lesson 14 funnel benchmarks).
- **Day 36-42**: First Fanvue subs trickle in. Sub price $4.99. Welcome message automated via Fanvue messaging. PPV-set #1 (5-10 images, $5) goes live Day 40.

### Week 7-8 — Paid ads + global seeding

- **Day 43-49**: Meta Ads turned on. Budget $20-30/day, single audience: 18-34 male, lookalike of current engagers, niche interest stack (Fanvue's existing creators + adjacent IG accounts). First-campaign target per Get-Ryze 2026 data: $1.50-3 CPM, $0.50-1 cost per landing-page view, $5-10 cost per Fanvue trial sub. https://www.get-ryze.ai/blog/how-much-spend-meta-ads-first-campaign-2026
- **Day 50-56**: Tinder Passport (Tinder Plus / Gold required) — seed presence in 4-5 high-LTV cities (NYC, LA, London, Sydney, Dubai). Bio = IG handle. Profile photo set = 6 best AI portraits. Daily Tinder activity 15-25 swipes/likes per city. https://tinderprofile.ai/blog/tinder-passport-guide/
- **Day 56**: ManyChat funnel V2 — add a follow-up sequence (Day 1 free preview, Day 3 first PPV nudge, Day 7 sub-now-or-lose-discount).

### Week 9-10 — Volume up, customs introduced

- **Day 57-63**: Reels at 3/day stabilize. Grid 4-5/week. Customs offered to Fanvue subs ($50-100 entry; lesson 14 has the curve). Live-chat sessions Saturday evenings (CET).
- **Day 64-70**: First $1k week becomes plausible: 100 active subs at $4.99 ($499 MRR) + 30 PPV sales at $10 avg ($300) + 5 customs at $80 avg ($400) = $1,199.

### Week 11-12 — Optimize, apply for ambassador

- **Day 71-77**: Cut bottom-quartile Reels formats. Double down on top performers. Test sub price bump to $9.99 (or grandfather existing at $4.99 and ratchet new sign-ups). A/B PPV pricing on next 2 sets.
- **Day 78-84**: Apply to Fanvue Ambassador / Top Creator program (typical entry: 30-day consistent posting + min revenue + verification). Approval typically arrives Week 13-14 and unlocks featured-discovery placement.

End of Day 90: target $1,000-2,500 MRR, 200-500 paid subs, 5-10k IG followers, 20-30 customs delivered, full funnel running on autopilot via ManyChat + Bouncy + Fanvue chat. Tier 2 by Fanvuebest distribution; Tier 3 trajectory if engagement holds.

## Quality benchmarks (per phase)

| Phase | Posts | Followers | Subs | MRR |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| End of Phase 1 (Day 14) | 14 grid + 3 reels | 200-500 | 0 | $0 |
| End of Phase 2 (Day 28) | 18+ reels live | 1-3k | 0-5 | $0-30 |
| End of Phase 3 (Day 42) | 3 reels/day rhythm | 3-6k | 20-50 | $100-300 |
| End of Phase 4 (Day 90) | sustained | 5-15k | 200-500 | $1k-2.5k |

Saves-to-likes ratio target ≥1:4. Comment response time <60 minutes (algorithm signal per Catalyst Communications 2026 IG algo update). Story completion >80%.

## Common failure modes + fixes

- **Posting Reels in Week 1** → kills warmup, locks reach. Wait until Day 11 minimum.
- **Adding link-in-bio before Day 29** → flagged as "spam-trajectory account" by IG's 2026 trust system. Hold the link.
- **Three different niches in the first 30 days** → algorithm can't classify; reach plateau permanent. Pick one niche, stay on it 90 days.
- **ManyChat keyword too generic** ("link") → ManyChat known issue (community thread Apr 2026): instability after 2-3 messages with generic triggers. Use niche-specific triggers ("menu", "drop", brand handle). https://community.manychat.com/general-q-a-43/instagram-dm-automation-unstable-after-2-3-messages-manychat-make-webhook-synchronous-issue-9521
- **Meta ads before Week 7** → wastes budget on a cold pixel; the Meta ad pixel needs 30-day organic data to optimize. https://influencerdb.net/paid-social/facebook-ad-campaign/
- **Tinder Passport without local-time alignment** → low Tinder visibility. Set passport timezone to seeded city; swipe at the city's 7-11pm peak.
- **Fanvue sub at $14.99 from Day 1** → conversion catastrophe. Use the Phase-3 lesson 14 curve.

## When to choose this over the course's recipe

- **Always**, for a single new creator launch. The course's pieces don't assemble into a calendar; this one does.
- **For agency runs (5+ creators)**, parallelize the calendar: each new creator on a 2-week stagger so Spark renders, ManyChat config, and ad-budget review aren't all on the same day.
- **Adjust** Phase 4 timing if Fanvue sign-up rate is below 50/week by Day 56 — extend Phase 3 organic for another 14 days before turning on paid ads.
- **Stick with Herman's Phase 4 "ad-running strategy is fucking insane"** energy as your push, but layer this calendar on it as the schedule.
