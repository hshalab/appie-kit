# Instagram Organic Content (Profile Funnel content engine)

Phase 2 organic layer: "Traffic & content is the key factor to success. White space on a calendar is the #1 thing to prevent." Goal = let content do the conversion so DMs fill with pre-sold leads.

## Bio optimization

Three lines: **direct headline → credibility/numbers → CTA to DM or value asset.**
- In-direct ROI niches (fitness, mindset): headline + "[X]+ people helped" + "DM 'word' for info".
- Direct ROI niches (finance, B2B): "I scale [niche] with [mechanism]" + revenue proof + "DM 'TOF' for the blueprint". At $30k-250k/mo, simplify the message ("Did $87k with consulting, now helping others build the back-end").
- Profile pic: define brand from 3 standpoints — visual (e.g. classy, dark, high-production), verbal (confidence, relatable), value (niche-specific, technical).

## The 3 reel types (every reel is one of these)

Always show a **screen** for value reels (doc, Miro, dashboard, software). Hook = max 4s, **lead with a painpoint not a profile type.**

| Type | Visual | Hook style | Body | CTA |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Value-driven** | Face + screen of a system/SOP/asset | Painpoint that opens the loop | Freestyle around the screen; conscious=show SOP, unconscious=speak with conviction | Comment "X" for the full asset |
| **Educational** | Mic/authority bg + charts | Controversial belief-breaker ("80% of ads fail before they run") | Painpoints (ICP-specific) → Solution; teach via examples/stats/stories | Hard / Soft / No CTA |
| **Credibility** | Stripe/Ads Manager/dashboards, client wins | Undeniable result, not your title ("7 clients crossed $100k in 60 days") | Painpoints framed through proof → how proof changes the game | Comment "PROGRESS" for breakdown |

Hook examples — wrong: "If you're a coach looking for…"; right: "Your leadflow is great but the quality sucks, here's why…". Unique "levers"/trademarks in your marketing beat copying competitors (short-term gain, long-term loss).

**Posting:** min 3x/week, 11am-2pm EST, rotate value/educational/credibility.

## Story sequences (the personal-touch converter)

"Adds ~1% conversion per sequence watched." 5 sequence types: **Q&A, Educational, Credibility, Value, Single.** Post all slides at once, 11am-2pm EST.

- **Educational sequence (6 slides):** 1 Curiosity → 2 Obstacle (make them feel the pain) → 3 Statement (credibility + data) → 4 Belief (shift their mechanism view) → 5 Transition (where you were → where you are) → 6 Trust (act from abundance, often NO CTA: "we're full, will let you know").
- **Credibility sequence (5 slides):** 1 Proof hook (undeniable result + screenshot) → 2 First win → 3 Stack wins → 4 Authority perspective (today's snapshot + scarcity) → 5 Transition.
- **Value sequence (6 slides):** 1 Context (scene/character) → 2 Build-up (tension/conflict + action) → 3-5 Frame (deliver the value, 1-3 slides) → 6 Mechanism + CTA ("reply BLUEPRINT for the 20-min YT breakdown").

**Audience tuning:** B2C balance lifestyle + educational (buy on emotion); B2B balance credibility + value (buy on logic/data). Consistency + data beats perfectionism — don't overthink timing/positioning.

**Sample week:** Mon Credibility · Tue Value + lifestyle single · Wed Educational · Thu Mix single · Fri off · Sat Credibility + CTA single · Sun Educational + talking head.

## Content-idea engine

Pick a niche-important topic (team, ads, lead-gen, content, fulfillment). Sub-divide one topic (e.g. "ads") into 8-10 sub-angles. Each sub-angle can ship as a reel AND as value/credibility/educational stories. 10+ pieces in 3 minutes.

## Common mistakes

- Opening a hook with a profile type ("if you're a coach…") instead of a painpoint.
- Value reels with no on-screen artifact (no doc/dashboard/software shown).
- Posting one content type only — the system needs value + educational + credibility in rotation.
- Hard CTA on every story; trust-building (no CTA / "we're full") often converts harder.
- Chasing perfect sequences over consistent posting + reading the data.
